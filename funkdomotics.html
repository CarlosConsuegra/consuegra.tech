What is Funkdomotics?

Funkdomotics is a comprehensive smart home and building automation platform that integrates all your devices into one unified, intelligent system. Built on open-source foundations and cutting-edge technology, we create automation solutions that are reliable, scalable, and completely customizable to your needs.
